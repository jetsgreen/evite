Our Story

Where our story began… from Maria’s point of view haha

One day I decided to organize a Paintball outing for close friends and family, which happen to include Paula & Jonathan (my aunt & her husband). We were supposed to leave at 7 o’clock, but Paula wouldn’t be her if she wasn’t running late . As I grew tired of waiting for her, I rushed into her home ready to cause a scene but as soon as I charged through the door I stopped to see an unknown man sitting casually on the sofa. This man stood up and politely introduced himself. His name was Felix – Hello Felix. Who was he? Where did he come from and why haven’t I met him before!? All questions were running through my mind and I made sure to quiz Paula – forget about her running late.

We finally were on our way to battle the apocalyptic zombies and everyone was pretty excited. While we were there I accidently misfired and shot Paula in the neck, which took our outing from 100 to 0 REAL quick – oops . We all retreated back to Paula & Jonathan’s house for pizza, wine, & American Horror Story, Season 4 – if you know me, you KNOW, this is one of my favorite shows. A night in with good vibes, wine, and the mysterious Felix sounded good to me! That night ended with talks about the show & me giving Felix directions back home haha.

I admit, I wanted to get to know him more but I thought – “alright, I’ll see him when I see him”. Low and behold, guess who had a DM the very next day. At first, I was a bit hesitant to open it because I didn’t know who Felior21 was but that was one of the best decisions I’ve ever made. He learned the night before about my fear of clowns and he thought it’d be amusing to say “BOO!” with a picture of Pennywise the Clown, from IT as his very first message to me. I replied with a “I hate you -_- and I didn’t ask for help with my phobia of clowns”. I probably should’ve ended the statement with an LOL but I wouldn’t be me if I wasn’t a little spicy. ;)

The rest was history – I still remember our first date. It was at Euro’s Café with a BYOB menu and we shared a hookah. From that moment on, I knew something was different about him – something more to explore, something exciting. Almost 6 years later and we’ve been inseparable, we’ve created love, memories, and a beautiful family. The day has come for us to finally become one. I couldn’t be happier to spend the rest of my life with my best friend.
